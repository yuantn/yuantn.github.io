Biography

I am a master student in Pattern Recognition and Intelligent System Development Laboratory (PriSDL) at University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS), advised by Prof. Qixiang Ye. I got a B.E. degree in Electronic Information Science and Technology at Tsinghua University (THU) in July 2019.

My research interests include computer vision and machine learning, specifically for active learning and feature learning.
