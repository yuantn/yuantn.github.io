Biography

I am an Algorithm Engineer at Noah's Ark Lab in Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd.. My project leader is Xinghao Chen. I received my M.Eng. degree advised by Prof. Qixiang Ye in Electronics and Communication Engineering at University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) in July 2022, and my B.Eng. degree advised by Prof. Huimin Ma in Electronic Information Science and Technology at Tsinghua University (THU) in July 2019.

I was an intern at Noah's Ark Lab in Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd. from Jun. - Dec. in 2021. My mentor was Songcen Xu.

My research interests include Computer Vision and Machine Learning, specifically for Active Learning and Visual Object Detection. I am opening to all other interesting topics.
