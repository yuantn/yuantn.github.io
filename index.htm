Biography

I am a master candidate in Pattern Recognition and Intelligent System Development Laboratory (PriSDL) at University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS), supervised by Prof. Qixiang Ye. I got a B.E. degree in Electronic Information Science and Technology at Tsinghua University (THU) in July 2019.

My research interests include computer vision and machine learning, specifically for active learning, feature learning and visual object detection. I am opening to all other interesting topics.
